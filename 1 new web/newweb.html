Who We Are

A Legacy of Quality Education

Shristi English Medium School, Lambhvel has been a beacon of academic excellence, moral values, and holistic development for over two decades. We are committed to empowering every child with knowledge, skills, and confidence.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops intellectual curiosity and character.

🌟 Our Vision

To be a leading school nurturing responsible, innovative global citizens.

📚 Curriculum

GSEB & CBSE-aligned curriculum with modern, activity-based learning methods.

🏅 Co-curricular

Sports, arts, science fairs, cultural events and community programs.

Our Core Pillars

We believe education is not just about academics — it is about shaping confident, compassionate, and curious human beings ready for tomorrow's challenges.

  • English Medium instruction from Nursery to 10th
  • Smart classrooms with modern technology
  • Library, Science & Computer Labs
  • Safe and child-friendly campus
  • Regular Parent–Teacher interactions
  • Annual Sports Day, Cultural Programs
  • Scholarship opportunities for meritorious students
