Legacy of Excellence Since 1961

🏫 Our History

Smt. M.M. Bhakta High School, Netrang was established in 1961 with a vision to provide quality education to the students of Netrang and surrounding areas in Bharuch district, Gujarat. The school was founded with a commitment to academic excellence, discipline, and moral values.

Named after the esteemed Smt. Maniben Maganbhai Bhakta, the institution has been a beacon of education for over six decades. What started as a small educational initiative has grown into a renowned higher secondary school offering education from Standard 9 to 12.

Over the years, our school has produced thousands of successful alumni who have excelled in various fields including engineering, medicine, civil services, business, and arts. The founding batch of 1961 laid the foundation of the traditions and values that continue to define our institution today.

The school has continuously evolved to meet the changing educational needs while maintaining its core values of discipline, integrity, and academic excellence. Today, we offer multiple streams including Science, Commerce, Arts, and SSC to cater to the diverse aspirations of our students.
