📋 Annual Academic Plan

April - June 2025: Foundation & Opening

• School reopening & orientation for new students
• Diagnostic tests & academic planning
• Curricular activities & sports training begins
• First term assignments & projects

July - August 2025: Regular Classes

• Regular classroom teaching & learning
• Mid-term assessment & feedback
• Lab work & practical training
• Cultural programs & activities

September 2025: Half Yearly Exams

• Half yearly examination (15-28 Sept)
• Pre-exam revision & practice sessions
• Counseling & guidance for students
• Parent-teacher meetings

October - December 2025: Second Phase

• Post-exam analysis & remedial teaching
• Continuation of syllabus coverage
• Annual day preparations & celebrations
• Winter sports & events

January - March 2026: Final Exams

• Final syllabus completion
• Board exam preparation (Class 10, 12)
• Final year examinations (March 10-25)
• Board exams as per GSEB schedule

April 2026: Year Closure

• Result announcement & merit lists
• Awards & recognition ceremony
• Graduation ceremony for Class 10 & 12
• Summer vacation begins

📌 Note: This plan is provisional and may be modified based on official announcements, holidays, and unforeseen circumstances. Regular updates will be communicated through the notice board.
