Welcome to Our School

Established in 1961, Smt. M.M. Bhakta High School has been an institution of choice for quality education in Netrang, Bharuch. Over six decades, we have shaped thousands of students into responsible citizens with strong academics and ethical values.

Our school provides education from Standard 9 to 12 with specialized streams including Science, Commerce, and Arts. We emphasize holistic development through academic excellence, sports, cultural activities, and character building.
