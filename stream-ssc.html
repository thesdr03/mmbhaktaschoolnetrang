What is SSC Stream?

The SSC (Secondary School Certificate) Stream is designed for students in Classes 9 and 10. It provides a comprehensive, well-rounded curriculum that covers science, mathematics, social science, languages, and arts to prepare students for higher secondary streams.

Our goal is to develop a strong academic foundation while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and confidence in our students.
