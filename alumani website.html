About Us 🏫

Our Story & Values

🎯

Our Mission

To foster lifelong connections among alumni, support current students through mentorship, and contribute to the continuous growth of our beloved institution.

👁️

Our Vision

To create a globally connected alumni network that empowers graduates to achieve excellence and give back to society while honoring our shared heritage.

💎

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Unity, Service, and Lifelong Learning guide everything we do. We believe in nurturing bonds that transcend time and distance.

🏆

Notable Achievements

Our alumni have excelled in various fields including IAS officers, doctors, engineers, entrepreneurs, artists, and social workers making us proud worldwide.

🤝

Community Support

We actively support underprivileged students through scholarships, organize career guidance sessions, and maintain strong bonds with our alma mater.
