About Our School

Smt. M.M. Bhakta High School, Netrang is a prestigious educational institution, which provides quality education from standard 9 to 12. In our school, special emphasis is placed on culture, discipline and moral values ​​along with the academic development of the students. In standard 11 and 12, students are guided by modern knowledge and life values ​​through common stream. Our goal is to develop every student in an all-round way and prepare them for a bright future. Through experienced teachers, positive educational environment and modern teaching methods, we are committed to taking students towards success.

We believe in holistic development - nurturing not just academic excellence but also character, creativity, and leadership qualities in our students.
